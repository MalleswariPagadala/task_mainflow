Apple

Crunchy and sweet, apples are a healthy and refreshing snack!

Apples come in various varieties like Fuji, Gala, and Granny Smith. Rich in fiber and vitamin C.

Banana

Bananas are a great source of potassium and an excellent energy booster!

Bananas are rich in vitamins B6 and C, and they make a perfect snack for athletes and busy people.

Orange

Oranges are juicy and packed with vitamin C, perfect for boosting your immune system!

Oranges are not only delicious but also help in preventing colds and boosting skin health.

Strawberry

Sweet and tangy, strawberries are a favorite for smoothies and desserts!

Strawberries are full of antioxidants and vitamin C, and they're a great addition to any diet!
